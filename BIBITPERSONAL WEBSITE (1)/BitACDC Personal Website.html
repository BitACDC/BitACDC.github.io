ABOUT ME

Dice Hard was a game that me and my friends developed for one of our

major final projects. It was a dungeon crawling game inspired by games

like Pokemon Mystery Dungeon and Nethack. The game has a unique

dice mechanic where the player’s actions are dependent on the amount of

dice points you have in order to win you have to go through 5 dungeon

levels.
